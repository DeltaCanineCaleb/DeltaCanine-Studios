Download! (Mac)

Funky Cube Dash is a very simple game, where you, a cube dashes through the level.
And there's funky 3D physics involved, hence the name Funky Cube Dash.
This game was built in Unity using Brackeys' "How to Make a Game" Tutorial, and expanded upon once I got the basics down.

  • Run through 11 unique levels!
  • Simplistic appearance with easy controls
  • Wacky physics that are a joy to interact with
  • A pleasant soundtrack that gives the game that little extra something
  • Uh... a very good game! (At least I think so.)
